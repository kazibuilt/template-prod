An OS for the world.Cosmos is the operating system for the Ai era, blending intelligent technologies with intuitive interaction and advanced security. It's designed for simplicity, ensuring your digital life is effortless and feels as expansive as the cosmos itself. With Cosmos, tasks aren't just completed, they are transformed. All through an OS that's not just part of your world, but understands it.
Ai Bus: The Neural SuperhighwayCosmos introduces the Ai Bus, a groundbreaking Ai framework that transforms how cloud software interacts. As an ultra-fast conduit enabling Ai-driven experiences, the era of searching for, downloading, managing, or launching apps is over. Our Ai Bus quickly understands what you need, connecting you to the right Ai experience or service instantly, all at the speed of thought.
Context is KeyThe Context System goes beyond prediction. Our Ai Architecture comprehends the context of what you are saying, doing or holding to intelligently guide you to the best Ai experience for the task at hand. Ai Pin couples a deep understanding of the world with a deep understanding of you.
Gestures that go beyond touchNavigate through your digital world with the tilt and roll of a wrist or the closing of your fingers. Our gesture system offers precise and responsive command, making each interaction feel as natural as everyday movement. With Cosmos, your gestures directly translate into action, providing a seamless and efficient user experience.
