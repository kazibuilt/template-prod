Director, Electrical Engineering

Hardware at Humane in San Francisco, CA

Who We Are

Humane is a team of proven industry experts who have invented, built, and shipped category-defining hardware and software products to billions of people across the globe. We're known for building the audacious, ambitious, and the impossible, and we're doing it again. Our vision for the next shift between humans and computing requires innovation across multiple emerging technologies in hardware and software, from devices to cloud services.

About The Role

We are seeking a creative, passionate and experienced Electrical Engineering Director to lead Humane’s team of talented engineers. Our ideal candidate will have a strong background in electrical engineering, paired with proven leadership abilities. Your primary role will be to oversee the design, development, and implementation of electrical systems and products while ensuring the highest levels of quality and efficiency.

Humane's Electrical Engineering (EE) team works cross-functionally with our accomplished teams in Design, Software, Wireless, Program management, and Operations to deliver our products to market. As the Electrical Engineering Director in the Hardware Organization, you will manage the team responsible for electrical architecture, component selection, design and integration, validation, and manufacturing support of boundary-pushing cutting-edge products like Ai Pin and more.. 

What You Will Do 

  • Lead and manage the EE team's design and validation work. 
  • Oversee electrical design throughout a product design cycle, delivering results on-time. 
  • Work with internal cross-functional teams to successfully bring products to market.
  • Grow the EE team and internal capabilities to support the product roadmap. 
  • Coach team members to perform at their best level and provide opportunities for continual career development. 
  • Present project status to cross-functional leaders and executive team. 
  • Identify emerging technologies that will unlock new user experiences and features.
  • Contribute to and refine the product roadmap with cross functional partners.

Essential Qualifications

  • 10+ years of experience shipping high volume consumer electronics products, preferably with cellular connectivity. 
  • 5+ years of experience leading technical teams.
  • Excellent organizational and communication skills, and experience presenting to senior leadership teams.
  • Experience designing space-constrained and power-constrained products with microprocessors and Systems-on-a-Chip (SoCs).
  • Experience with I2C, SPI, UART, USB, MIPI, DSI communication protocols.
  • Experience designing system and development PCBs, flexes, and modules from concept to production, and executing board-level validation.
  • Experience with schematic capture and circuit board layout design tools.
  • Hands-on experience working with EE lab equipment for measuring system performance.
  • Experience working with overseas CMs/JDMs to design and manufacture products.

Preferred Skills

  • Experience working on cellular-connected wearable devices.
  • Experience with System-in-Package (SiP) design and manufacturing.
  • Experience developing and delivering a new technology or sensor to market. 
  • Passionate about working on highly creative, fast-paced, and collaborative teams alongside Wireless Engineers, Product Design Engineers, Software Engineers, and ID/UX Designers
  • Ability to travel to factories to support builds.
  • Ability to speak Mandarin is a plus.

Salary Range:  $240,000 - $285,000
